Welcome to my YouTube Channel: Rotomizer

Rotomizer was a name I made for myself when I was younger and it just kind of stuck with me. This YouTube channel serves as a space for me to showcase my creative talents and create music videos to share online.

I was inspired by many other online cover artists to start my own channel in 2019, and I've been putting out projects here and there since then.

My main focus is video game music and jazz, but I've produced recordings of other songs for college courses and put them here too. Feel free to watch here or check out my channel on YouTube!
