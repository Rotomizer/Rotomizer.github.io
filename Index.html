Andrew Clarke

Composer. Producer. Recording Artist. You name it.

Hi, I'm Andrew

I'm a Mexican-American multi-instrumentalist born in Texas with a background in arranging and recording. I've been playing music since the 6th grade, and I have a deep passion for jazz, video game music, and latin-american culture. I can:

  • play trumpet, tenor saxophone, piano, guitar, and sing
  • arrange/orchestrate for large ensembles
  • score original compositions
  • mix studio and live recordings
  • organize and plan large-scale productions
  • edit videos and graphics
  • give a performance you won't forget

I like to learn about every aspect of the project each time I pursue a creative endeavor. Learning exactly how each piece of the puzzle fits into place, I believe, is what makes the difference and makes it all the more worth it.
