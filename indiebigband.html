This performance took place on December 7th, 2023 in the University Theatre at The University of Texas at Dallas. Over the course of four months, I arranged nine collections of various indie video game songs, directed a 31-piece jazz big band orchestra, and organized all the communication and planning that went into this show.

I demostrated determination, leadership, and passion fully in this performance. However, this concert would not have been possible without the efforts of all the people involved. My sincerest thanks goes out to everyone involved, and I hope we can work together again someday. All people directly involved can be found on pg. 2 of the Program. Photos by Evan Acuna.
