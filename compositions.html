Game Soundtrack - Tobor Quest (2022)

I composed the entire soundtrack for a game made by the AHT Game Production Lab course. The player plays as a food delivery robot that tries to get its order to the customer as fast as it can over the course of four levels. I made both a regular version of the soundtrack and a more retro-sounding "B side" for the game, which the player could switch between whenever they wanted. The game can be found on their website here.

Animation/Music Project - Sweater Weather (2023)

I composed an entirely original track that was paired up with an animation under the same theme of Sweater Weather. The track features midi versions of the bassoon, piano, and marimba. The track was played with the animation at a live showing on October 20th, 2023, on campus.
